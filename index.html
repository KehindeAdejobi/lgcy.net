DPoS Blockchain

LGCY Network uses Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS) consensus algorithm developed to secure a blockchain by ensuring representation of transactions within it.

Low Cost

Average cost per transaction are set at 0,01$ per TX. Ideal to build fast, low cost solidity dapps or run complex smart contracts.

Libertas Protocol

The Libertas Protocol splits up the 27 Governing Bodies (GB) into three branches, limiting the power of each, therefore no single large token holder will be able to gain an unbalanced amount of power.

Fast Network

LGCY's blockchain is capable of processing over 10,000 transactions per second, confirming transactions instantly. LRC-20 and LRC-721 tokens.

EVM Compatible

With EVM compatibility, developers can build the same way that they would do on Ethereum, and benefit from the same tools and applications.

Open Source

LGCY Network is open source and public available. Connect to and with LGCY's blockchain, readily available for dApps and smart contract launches instantaneously at a low cost.

USDL Stablecoin

Stablecoin used for GAS and within ecosystem as base pair

USDL is the gas token for LGCY Network. Instead of having bandwidth and energy, the LGCY Network will use USDL as gas. 1 USDL will be a credit for $1 worth of transaction/server fees. USDL is a stable coin. That means that for people who stake, or run nodes with LGCY tokens they will earn payouts in a safe, stable coin rather than in a volatile token.

21 Billion Total Supply

100 Billion lowered to 21 Billion. 33% of total supply are released for the team to keep the voting power and control over the network. These tokens will be burned to turn project over to the community and developers building on LGCY.

60% $LGCY in Circulation

No vested tokens. All tokens are in circulation to be used within the LGCY Network ecosystem.

Fund for dApp and Ecosystem

Remainder of the tokens will be egibile for developers building on LGCY Network. Kickstarting builders with funds and liquidity for within the LGCY ecosystem.

Leadership

LGCY Network was founded in August 2020, with the intent of delivering the promise of a truly decentralized blockchain.

At the beginning of the venture, centralization is required to get off the ground. As the network grows, however, the community will begin to take over the leadership of LGCY network. It is not our intention to remain in voting control forever. By handing over governance to the community, LGCY Network will be the most decentralized blockchain in existence.
